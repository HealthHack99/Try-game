About Us

Codehal! is a top gaming quiz. This is a great learning platform for intermediate students.

Codehal! comprises 5 main science subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Computer Science

Each subject option contains two levels:

  • Level 1 for beginners
  • Level 2 for advanced learners

After completing both levels, students will have a good understanding of the mentioned subjects. Let's try it out, and good luck with the quiz!
